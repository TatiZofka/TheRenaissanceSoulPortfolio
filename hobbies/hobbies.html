HOBBIES I AM STILL POLISHING

Hobbies are my sacred space, where I allow myself to make a lot of mistakes. Furthermore, most of my nowadays jobs started as a hobby and when I am starting a new activity, I never know if it will be just a temporary pass-time like playing ukulele or long-term love as my journaling obsession which led me to start my own sticker shop. Life has a million flavors and as a true Renaissance Soul, I am excited to taste many of them. I do not want to die with a feeling that I missed some experience and as long as I am having fun doing them, doesn't matter If I am actually good at any of them.
