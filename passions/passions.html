PASSIONS THAT DRIVE MY SOUL

I have never believed what the work-life equation represents. To me, work is just another part of life. I always have dedicated my free time to developing passions that keep my mind busy and my soul restless. Many of them have the potential to become my full-time paid job, although I prefer to switch it up every once and then. Thanks to that, I have never been fed up with them. When I am working on them, it doesn't feel like work and time flies by. These are the projects I am constantly returning to whenever I need to feel the spark of new ideas, fulfil my hunger for creativity,and see my visions becoming real.
